The Pattern

9 verified outcomes demonstrate real outcomes across standard market tiers. 7 aspirational cases show exceptional FAANG/unicorn-tier achievements possible with peak performance. Combined: 16 cases spanning $67K to $320K, 37 to 212 days, across all experience levels and specializations.

Most candidates fall in the verified tier ($67K-$185K). Aspirational outcomes ($245K-$320K) require exceptional technical depth, interview performance, and market timing. Both are valid paths.
