Common Questions

What counts as a qualifying interview?

+
A qualifying interview must be a technical screen or onsite with an $80K+ W-2 role at a company with 20+ employees. You must have legal work authorization in the target country.

When is the success fee due?

+
The success fee is due within 30 days of accepting a qualifying offer. Payment plans are available if needed. The fee applies only to your first-year base salary, not bonuses or equity.

How does the refund guarantee work?

+
If you do not receive at least your guaranteed number of qualifying interviews within 100 days, you may request a refund of your upfront payment anytime until day 180. See Refund Policy for complete terms.

Can I upgrade my plan after starting?

+
Yes, you can upgrade at any time by paying the difference in upfront cost. Your success fee rate will update to the new plan's rate for any future offers.
