What We Actually Do

We don't teach you to code. We don't give you "motivation." We don't sell you courses on "mindset." We don't run a bootcamp.

We build your portfolio projects (with you), submit your applications (for you), prep you for interviews (relentlessly), and guarantee interviews. Or refund every dollar.

We handle the soul-crushing parts so you can focus on the part that matters: being ready when the interview comes.

Our Principles

1. No Wasted Motion

Every hour you spend should increase your employability. If it doesn't directly lead to interviews or offers, we don't make you do it.

2. Portfolio Over Resume

Resumes are filtered by bots. Portfolio projects with real metrics (50K req/sec, 99.9% uptime, fraud detection accuracy) get humans interested.

3. We Eat Our Own Dog Food

Success-based pricing means we lose money if you don't get hired. We're not selling hope. We're betting on you.

4. Interviews > Offers

We guarantee interviews, not offers. Why? Because once you're in the room with real portfolio work and interview prep, you close the deal yourself. We get you the at-bats.

Why This Works

Application Volume: We submit a minimum of 30-75 applications on your behalf (varies by plan). You never touch a job board again.

Portfolio Proof: When hiring managers see a production system you built (with metrics, deployment, monitoring), they stop caring about gaps in your resume.

Interview Readiness: Mock interviews, system design drills, behavioral prep. You walk into rooms confident because you've done this 20 times already.

Aligned Incentives: We only get paid when you get hired. If this doesn't work, we lose more than you do.

Who We're Not For

If you're brand new to tech and can't code yet, learn the basics first, then come back.

If you want "passive income" or "get rich quick," this is 100 days of real work.

If you're employed and just shopping around, you don't need this. You need salary negotiation tactics.

This is for unemployed or underemployed people who can do the work but can't get past the gatekeepers. That's our lane.
